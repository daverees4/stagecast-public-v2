Your new digital performance platform

 
 

We believe experiencing a concert online needn’t be second best to being there in person. That’s why we’re developing a new platform to allow orchestras, concert halls, festivals, theatre companies and other performers to reach audiences in new ways.

Top notch pictures and sound are just the start. Moving beyond established approaches to live streaming, Stagecast offers unprecedented interactivity, immersion, context and community interaction to define a new model for digital performance and engagement.

Starting with a simple ‘white label’ platform integrated into your existing website, we can provide all the tools and expertise you need to get your performances out there in a way which builds engagement with your existing audience – and attracts new ones.
